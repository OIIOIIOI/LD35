PRESS SPACE TO START/RESTART
USE THE ARROW KEYS TO MOVE

Try to last as long as possible by avoiding or destroying the moving Shapes.
The flashing area around yourself burns and destroys Shapes but the white circle in the center is vulnerable.
Orange and Red Shapes split into too smaller Shapes when destroyed.
